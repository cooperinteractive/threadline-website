QR Smart Tags — The Product Play
Product + Revenue Feature

This isn't just swag. It's a consumable that drives app adoption.

Pre-printed hang tags with sequential QR codes (TL-00001 through TL-00500). Each tag is "dumb" until someone scans it with the app. At that point, THREADLINE says "this tag isn't linked to anything yet, photograph the garment now." Snap the photo, the QR gets bound to that inventory item. From that point on, anyone on the team can scan that tag and pull up the full item record: store, price, character, return deadline, photos, everything.

Once a production's rack is tagged with THREADLINE QR tags, they're not switching apps. The physical tags create lock-in that no software feature alone can match.

How It Works

1
Hang the tag
Attach a QR tag to any garment on the rack. The tag has a unique code but isn't linked to anything yet.
2
Scan with the app
Point THREADLINE's camera at the QR code. The app detects it's unlinked and prompts you to photograph the garment.
3
Tag is now bound
The QR code is permanently linked to that inventory item. Store, price, character, return deadline, all attached to that tag.
4
Anyone can scan
Any team member scans the tag to instantly pull up the full item record. No searching, no scrolling, no guessing.

Your cost is roughly $25-30 per 250 with variable QR printing. Break-even or small margin, but the real value is retention and lock-in.

Variable Data Printing Cost

Sequential QR codes add roughly 10-15% over static tags. At volume (500+), you're looking at $0.12-0.18 per tag with unique QR codes vs $0.10-0.15 for identical tags. The QR generation is just data, any commercial printer with variable data capability can handle it. Jukebox Print and 4over both support variable data runs.

Tag Lifecycle

Tags are "one item, one tag." When an item is returned or wrapped, the tag can be unlinked in the app and reattached to a new garment. If a tag gets damaged or lost, the item record still exists in the app. The physical tag is a convenience layer, not the source of truth.
