ThreadLine is built for the people who keep productions looking right: wardrobe production assistants, costume supervisors, and wardrobe shoppers working in film and television. About ThreadLine

ThreadLine is an AI-powered wardrobe management platform purpose-built for film and television production. It replaces the paper receipts, manual spreadsheets, and guesswork that wardrobe departments have relied on for decades. With intelligent scanning, automated return tracking, real-time budget reporting, and a conversational AI assistant, ThreadLine gives wardrobe professionals their time back so they can focus on what they do best: making productions look incredible.
