Long Description
Threadline is wardrobe management software built specifically for the film and television industry. Wardrobe departments run on paper, spreadsheets, and memory. Receipts get lost. Return deadlines slip. Wrapping a show means weekends buried in data entry. We built Threadline to fix that. Scan a barcode and inventory auto-fills. Snap a receipt and every line item is captured. When a return deadline is approaching, you get a heads-up before it is too late. At wrap, your paperwork is already done. From rack to wrap, Threadline keeps your wardrobe department organized so you can focus on the work that actually matters.
Mission Statement
To give wardrobe professionals the modern tools they deserve, so they can spend less time on paperwork and more time on the craft.
Company Overview
Threadline is a software company focused on production workflow tools for the entertainment industry. Our first product is an AI-powered wardrobe management app designed for film and TV wardrobe departments. We are currently in beta and building toward a public launch.
Where these go: On your Facebook page, go to Settings → Page Info. The Short Description appears under your page name in search results and the About preview. Facebook vs. Instagram: Facebook posts do not use hashtags the same way. Skip the hashtag block here. The algorithm favors shares and comments over hashtag discovery.
