Page AdminsAdd yourself as Super Admin. Add any co-founders as Content Admins.
First week strategy. Post the launch announcement. Invite your connections to follow the page (LinkedIn gives you a limited number of monthly invites, so start with people in film, TV, and production). Engage on posts from wardrobe professionals, production companies, and costume design accounts. Comment thoughtfully on industry conversations. Follow relevant companies and hashtags. LinkedIn rewards consistent, authentic engagement over volume.
