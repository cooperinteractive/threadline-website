250-Word Description

Press releases, investor materials, partnership decks

PullSheet is the visual lookbook builder designed for wardrobe professionals in film and television production. It transforms the way costume supervisors, set costumers, and wardrobe shoppers present their work to directors, department heads, and talent.

The app provides a freeform drag-and-drop canvas where garments can be arranged, resized, rotated, and layered into complete outfit compositions. Every garment photo can be cleaned up with one-tap AI background removal, turning phone photos taken on a crowded rack into catalog-quality cutouts.

PullSheet connects directly to THREADLINE, the companion wardrobe inventory management app. Every garment you have catalogued in THREADLINE is immediately available in PullSheet. No duplicate data entry, no manual imports. The apps share the same database, the same login, and the same production context.

Export options include single-look PDFs and multi-page lookbooks with cover pages, tables of contents, talent organization, and detailed garment lists with pricing. The result is a professional document you can hand to a director, email to a department head, or print for a fitting room wall.

Lookboards are organized by talent, scene, and episode, making it easy to build continuity bibles and track wardrobe progression across a production. Auto-save ensures no work is lost, and an undo/redo system lets you experiment freely.

PullSheet is built by Cooper Interactive and launches Summer 2026. It is available as a companion add-on to THREADLINE subscriptions.
