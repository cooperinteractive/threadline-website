07 / Brand Voice
Visual
We communicate through images first. The lookboard speaks louder than a paragraph.

PullSheet is a visual tool for visual people. Every interface decision favors showing over telling. If a picture can replace a sentence, it should.

Confident
We know what we do. Short sentences. Direct language. No hedging.

Film and television wardrobe professionals have no patience for vague marketing. PullSheet speaks plainly about what it does and why it matters.

Minimal
Every word earns its place. If it doesn't add clarity, it doesn't belong.

Like a well-edited lookboard, PullSheet's voice removes everything that doesn't serve the message. Clean. Clear. Done.
