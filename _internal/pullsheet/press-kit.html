About PullSheet

PullSheet is the visual lookbook builder for wardrobe teams in film and TV production. Drag garments from your inventory onto a freeform canvas, remove messy rack backgrounds with one-tap AI, and arrange outfits the way you think about them. Export single looks or multi-page lookbooks as print-ready PDFs, complete with cover pages, tables of contents, and garment details. Built on THREADLINE inventory data, PullSheet connects seamlessly to your existing wardrobe catalog. No re-scanning, no re-entering data. Filter by talent, scene, or episode. Hand the director a professional lookbook instead of a stack of Polaroids.

Key Features
  • Drag-and-Drop Canvas
    Freeform canvas with resize, rotate, and layer controls. Arrange garments exactly how you envision the outfit.
  • AI Background Removal
    One tap removes messy rack backgrounds from garment photos. Powered by Clipdrop for clean, professional compositions.
  • Connected to THREADLINE
    Pull garments directly from your THREADLINE inventory. Zero re-entry, zero duplicate scanning.
  • PDF Export
    Export single looks or multi-page lookbooks as print-ready PDFs with cover pages, tables of contents, and garment details.
  • Organized by Talent & Scene
    Filter and sort by talent, scene, and episode. Find the right look instantly across an entire production.
  • Team Collaboration
    Shared production access so the entire wardrobe team works from the same lookbooks. No version confusion.
  • Auto-Save with Undo/Redo
    Every change saved automatically. Full undo/redo history so you can experiment freely without losing work.
  • iPad-First Design
    Built for iPad with a side-panel garment picker. Also works on iPhone (bottom sheet) and desktop (planned).
Target Audience
Costume supervisors, set costumers, wardrobe shoppers, and assistant costume designers working in film and TV production.
Competitive Landscape

No production-grade visual lookbook builder exists in the market. Dress Assistant (dressassistant.com) is the closest competitor but lacks production awareness, AI background removal, and PDF export. Current workflows rely on printed photos, physical corkboards, and manual assembly.

Company
About Cooper Interactive

Cooper Interactive builds production tools for the entertainment industry. The company's flagship products, THREADLINE and PullSheet, serve wardrobe departments in film and television. THREADLINE handles wardrobe inventory management (scanning, tracking, returns), while PullSheet handles visual presentation (lookbooks, pull sheets, outfit planning).
