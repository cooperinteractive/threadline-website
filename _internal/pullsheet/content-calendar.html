Week 3

Days 15 - 19 · Community & Context
Day 15 · Monday
Industry Context
LI BTS / On-Set
LinkedIn Copy
The film industry has digitized nearly every department. Cameras are digital. Editing is digital. VFX, color, sound, scheduling, budgets, call sheets. All digital. But walk into a wardrobe truck on most productions and you'll find binders of Polaroids, handwritten tags, and spreadsheets that haven't been updated since last Friday. Wardrobe professionals manage thousands of garments across dozens of talent across entire seasons of television. They track purchases, rentals, returns, alterations, and continuity. They build lookboards for director approvals. They maintain records that determine whether a production gets its deposit back. And most of them are doing all of this with tools that were designed for something else entirely. We're building tools designed specifically for this workflow. THREADLINE handles inventory, scanning, and tracking. PullSheet handles visualization and presentation. Together, they're the wardrobe management stack that this industry has been waiting for.
