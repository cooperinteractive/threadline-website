Facebook vs. Instagram: Facebook posts do not use hashtags the same way. Skip the hashtag block here. The algorithm favors shares and comments over hashtag discovery. Write like a human, keep it conversational, and include the link directly in the post. Facebook does not penalize outbound links in page posts the way it does in personal posts.
