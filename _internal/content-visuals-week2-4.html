Why Wardrobe Budgets Go Over (And How to Prevent It)
THREADLINE
threadlineapp.io
Industry Insights
The 4 Invisible Budget Killers
⏰
Missed Return Deadlines
$50 rental becomes $400 purchase. Across 20 items = $7,000 over budget.
🔁
Duplicate Purchases
Without real-time visibility, two people buy the same item on the same day.
📄
Receipt Gaps
Missing receipts show up as unexplained expenses in the final budget report.
📈
Scope Creep
Script changes mean new purchases. Without live tracking, nobody sees the overage building.

Wardrobe Survival Kit
💼
Save This for Your Next Show
The Wardrobe
Survival Kit
Things nobody tells you on your first day.
SWIPE →
THREADLINE
@threadline_app
1
Photograph both the price tag AND the garment
Before removing any tags. Future you will thank present you.
2
Dedicated phone folder for continuity
Name photos by character and scene number, not just date. Finding "IMG_4782" three weeks later helps nobody.
THREADLINE
@threadline_app
3
Return receipts are just as important
File them separately and note which return goes with which original purchase.
4
Set return reminders immediately
The day you make the purchase. Not later. Not tomorrow. Right then.
THREADLINE
@threadline_app
5
Label everything
If you think you'll remember which rack is for which character, you won't. Not by day three.
6
Back up your spreadsheets daily
To the cloud. Every single night. No exceptions.
THREADLINE
@threadline_app

will handle most of
this automatically.
Until then, the survival kit stands.
