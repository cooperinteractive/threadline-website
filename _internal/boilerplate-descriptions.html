250-Word Description

ThreadLine is the first AI-powered wardrobe management platform designed specifically for the film and television industry. Built by Cooper Interactive, ThreadLine transforms how wardrobe departments operate -- replacing the clipboards, spreadsheets, and shoeboxes of receipts that have defined the role for decades.

The wardrobe truck just went digital.

At the heart of ThreadLine is Magic Scan, a suite of intelligent capture tools that dramatically accelerate data entry. Point your camera at a barcode for instant product lookup. Photograph a garment and let AI identify its type, color, brand, and key attributes. Snap a receipt and watch OCR extract every line item automatically. What once took hours of meticulous paperwork now takes seconds.

Beyond scanning, ThreadLine delivers a comprehensive production wardrobe toolkit: real-time inventory management organized by character, scene, and continuity; returns tracking with countdown timers that ensure nothing slips past a deadline; and integrated budget monitoring that gives supervisors instant visibility into department spending across vendors and episodes.

Thread AI, the platform's built-in assistant, understands production-specific workflows and can answer questions, generate reports, and surface insights from your wardrobe data -- like an experienced colleague who never forgets a detail.

ThreadLine runs natively on iPad (the primary device on set), iPhone (for quick reference on the go), and desktop web (for office-based supervisors and coordinators). Full offline mode ensures uninterrupted access during location shoots where connectivity is unreliable.

Designed for costume supervisors, wardrobe teams, and shoppers, ThreadLine reclaims the 80% of the workday currently lost to paperwork -- letting wardrobe professionals focus on the creative work that brought them to the industry. Follow @threadline_app on Instagram for updates.
